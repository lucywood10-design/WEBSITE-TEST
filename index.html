Philosophy

I am an architect working to challenge the dominant systems that shape how we build, own, and care for buildings in the UK.

My practice extends beyond designing buildings to designing systems. Hover over the pillars below to explore my core philosophy.

Systems Thinking

Trained at the Centre for Alternative Technology. I consistently question not just how we build, but why, for whom, and who is responsible over time.

Stewardship

Designing systems of participation and long-term care. Rooted in the belief that buildings are long-term social and environmental assets, not one-off products.

Material Innovation

Extensive hands-on experience with natural materials and modern methods of construction (MMC), balancing ecological impact with practical buildability.
