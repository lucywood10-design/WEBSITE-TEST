Philosophy

I am an architect working to challenge the dominant systems that shape how we build, own, and care for buildings in the UK.

My practice extends beyond designing buildings to designing systems of stewardship, participation, and long-term care. Rooted in the belief that buildings are a long-term social and environmental asset, not a one-off product.

Trained at the Centre for Alternative Technology and working professionally across housing delivery, natural materials, and construction systems, I combine architectural expertise with systems thinking. I consistently question not just how we build, but why, for whom, and who is responsible over time.
