Work I've Shaped

7 Homes for the Homeless

A pioneering social housing project delivering seven homes to expand Places for People's existing hostel. We navigated a challenging 40-degree slope and complex stakeholder requirements. We successfully utilized natural materials within the panelised construction. The project translates ambitious sustainability (social and environemntal) into actionable reality, proving that natural materials can be deployed effectively within tight budgets and that those moving in could live with dignity.
